Artist Statement

My writing leans on emotion, whether it be poetry, digital literature, or my favourite medium, short story. It is through emotion that I pursue the reason we, as humans, read what others write.

About Me

Hi! My name's Xavier and I'm a pretty dorky university student from RMIT. I have a huge passion for writing; my current focus is on short stories, but I have experience with all sorts of writing including screenwriting, digital literature, and poetry.

I first discovered my love of writing in year 10 through my fabulous English teacher Mrs. Rowston. She was the best teacher I ever had, and luckily enough I had her for 4 whole years. She discovered my talent for crafting a story that the reader can fall into. I really have her to thank for where I am now; she pushed me to write and pursue my love for the craft.

Both in reading and writing, my love falls to fantasy. The Hobbit, Sabriel, Harry Potter, I devoured all the fantasy books I could get my hands on as a child, and that comes out in my writing now. My style and my interest lends itself to fantasy in all of my fiction writing.

My interests outside of writing involve a wide range of video games, field hockey, and art. Hockey taught me how to work completely independently, take a role of leadership, work well in a team, and push myself to achieve a goal through umpiring, coaching, captaining, and training. These are life lessons I continue to take with me everywhere.
